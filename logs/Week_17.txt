Week 17 — Rule Expansion and Runtime Validation Refinement

Results demonstrated strong precision for several performance anti-pattern rules including SELECT *, non-sargable predicates, order-by sorting, and complex joins, while structural rules provided additional descriptive insight into query composition.

These results strengthen the evaluation component of the QueryLens analysis framework and support final project documentation.
